Fanttest Product Line-up

Current Fanttest product promoted by Rohee Medic in Malaysia.

(10 IN 1) COVID-19/hMPV/RSV/ADV/Flu A&B/HPIV1+3/HPIV2/MP/RhV Antigen Combo Rapid Test Kit

Lateral flow immunoassay for qualitative detection of:

  • COVID-19 (SARS-CoV-2)
  • Respiratory syncytial virus (RSV)
  • Adenovirus (ADV)
  • Influenza A & B
  • Parainfluenza 1+3 and Parainfluenza 2
  • Human metapneumovirus (hMPV)
  • Mycoplasma pneumoniae (MP)
  • Rhinovirus (RhV)

The test uses nasopharyngeal swab samples and is intended to support diagnosis of patients who meet case definitions for COVID-19 and other targeted respiratory infections within the specified symptom windows. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

  • Multi-pathogen respiratory screening in one cassette
  • Supports differential diagnosis when symptoms overlap
  • Designed for use in professional healthcare environments
