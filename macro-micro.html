About Macro & Micro-Test

A focused range of rapid diagnostic kits designed to support fast, reliable clinical decisions at the point of care.

The Macro & Micro-Test portfolio covers high-impact infectious disease segments – including respiratory diseases and dengue – with test formats suitable for hospital, clinic and laboratory environments.

Respiratory Panels

Multi-pathogen detection in a single test, designed to support triage and differential diagnosis for respiratory infections.

Dengue & Febrile Illness

Dengue NS1 and IgG/IgM combination kits to help clinicians assess infection phase and guide management.

Macro & Micro-Test Product Line-up

Overview of the key Macro & Micro-Test rapid kits currently promoted by Rohee Medic.

(6 in 1) SARS-CoV-2, Influenza A&B, RSV, ADV & MP Combined Detection Kit (Latex Method)

Multi-pathogen respiratory panel for qualitative detection of SARS-CoV-2, influenza A & B, respiratory syncytial virus, adenovirus and Mycoplasma pneumoniae from nasal and nasopharyngeal swab samples.

  • Supports differential assessment across key respiratory pathogens
  • Latex-based rapid format suitable for frontline screening
  • Intended for professional in vitro diagnostic use

MDA Registration No: IVDC4863224-186042

Dengue NS1 Antigen, IgM/IgG Antibody Dual

Rapid immunochromatographic test for qualitative detection of dengue NS1 antigen and IgM/IgG antibodies in serum, plasma or whole blood samples to support dengue diagnosis and phase evaluation.

  • NS1 + IgM/IgG in a single kit
  • Suitable for early and later phase dengue assessment
  • Designed for professional in vitro diagnostic use

MDA Registration No: IVDC3471024-170603

Download Brochure (PDF) Enquire Now

SARS-CoV-2 Virus Antigen Detection Kit (Colloidal Gold Method) – Nasal

Rapid test for qualitative detection of SARS-CoV-2 antigen from anterior nasal swab samples. Intended for self-testing (for users ≥15 years) or adult-assisted testing for younger individuals, as per the product instructions.

  • Anterior nasal swab sampling
  • Colloidal gold-based rapid result
  • Home-use self-testing and assisted testing (per IFU)

MDA Registration No: IVDC6120324-170603
