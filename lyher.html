Lyher Rapid Tests

About Lyher

Lyher offers rapid immunoassays covering respiratory infections, dengue and pregnancy testing, designed to provide clear results within minutes.

The test kits are configured for nasopharyngeal, oropharyngeal or whole blood/serum/plasma samples, depending on the assay, and are intended for in vitro diagnostic use in professional or self-test settings, as specified in each product’s instructions for use.

Respiratory & Febrile Illness

Combo panels for COVID-19 and influenza A/B, and dedicated influenza A/B kits to support respiratory triage and outbreak response.

Dengue & Women’s Health

Dengue NS1/IgG/IgM combo tests and hCG pregnancy strip/midstream tests for rapid diagnostics in clinical or pharmacy environments.

Lyher COVID-19 & Influenza A/B Antigen Test Kit

In vitro immunoassay for direct, qualitative detection of SARS-CoV-2 and influenza A/B antigens from nasopharyngeal or oropharyngeal secretions. Evaluated locally with high reported sensitivity and specificity.

  • CE marked
  • Results in ~15 minutes
  • Easy-to-read results
  • Swab depth approx. 2–3.5 cm
  • Room temperature storage
